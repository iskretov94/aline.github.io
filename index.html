About The School

Voice of Success School is where professionals, mostly actors and business people, go to learn the fundamentals of voice training like breathing, posture, and diction. These basic principles are key to strong vocal performances and successful business. Our expert coaches guide you through pitch training, public communication, and steady tempo. We build proficiency through our proprietary Drama School Method and theatre performances.

Voice Training Course

Designed for actors and performers wishing to refine their vocal ability, this course provides solid technical foundations. By the end, you will feel confident continuing your vocal development independently.

  • Coordinate breath and body for steady sound
  • Reduce unnecessary tension in the jaw and throat
  • Expand vocal range and experiment with resonance
  • Strengthen projection while maintaining clarity

Executive Voice Training

Your voice is a reflection of your authority and leadership. This course helps you develop a voice that carries strength, composure, and command in high-level business environments.

  • Project authority without strain
  • Command attention in boardrooms
  • Deliver presentations with confidence
  • Develop a deeper, more assured tone
